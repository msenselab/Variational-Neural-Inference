# Source Attribution & Credits

## Primary Source: Stanford STATS 320

All foundational materials in this library derive from or are adapted from **Stanford STATS 320: Machine Learning Methods for Neural Data Analysis**.

### Course Information
- **Official Website**: https://slinderman.github.io/ml4nd/
- **Instructor**: Scott Linderman (Stanford University)
- **Institution**: Stanford Department of Statistics
- **Course Level**: Graduate (PhD students in Statistics, CS, Neuroscience)

### Materials from Stanford Course
The following materials are adapted from or derived from official Stanford course content:

#### Core Lecture Notebooks
- Probabilistic modeling foundations
- Hidden Markov Models and Linear Dynamical Systems  
- Variational Expectation-Maximization
- LFADS (Latent Factor Analysis via Dynamical Systems)
- Mixture models and clustering

#### Theory & Derivations
- Mathematical frameworks for latent variable models
- Variational inference theory
- Expectation-Maximization algorithm
- Sequential VAE formulation

#### References & Datasets
- Curated bibliography of foundational papers
- Example datasets and synthetic data generation code
- Visualization techniques for neural data
